History of the bakery

Established in 2000, Cat'te Bakery has been a cherished landmark in the heart of Houston for 24 years, delighting locals and visitors alike with its irresistible array of freshly baked goods. Our journey began with a simple dream—to create a haven where people could gather, share stories, and savor the simple pleasures of life. Founded by a passionate baker with a vision, Cat'te Bakery quickly became known for its commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and community. From the very beginning, we have upheld the timeless art of baking, using only the finest ingredients and traditional techniques passed down through generations. But Cat'te Bakery is more than just a purveyor of delicious baked goods—it's a place where memories are made, friendships are forged, and moments are savored. Whether you're stopping by for your morning coffee and croissant or indulging in a slice of our famous homemade pie, every visit to Cat'te Bakery is an experience to be treasured. Thank you for being a part of our story. Whether you're a longtime patron or a first-time visitor, we invite you to join us on a journey of taste, tradition, and togetherness at Cat'te Bakery—where every day begins with a smile and a delicious bite.

Mission & Values

At Cat'te Bakery, our mission is to delight our customers with exceptional baked goods crafted with passion, tradition, and the finest ingredients. We are dedicated to creating a warm and welcoming environment where people can gather, connect, and savor the simple joys of life.

We are committed to excellence in every aspect of our craft, from the ingredients we use to the techniques we employ. We take pride in the quality of our baked goods and strive to exceed our customers' expectations with every bite.

We believe in the power of community and the importance of fostering meaningful connections. As a beloved local bakery, we are dedicated to supporting and enriching the communities we serve through partnerships, events, and initiatives that bring people together.

Meet the Team

Founder & Head Baker: Alejandra

With a lifelong passion for baking and a vision to create a warm and inviting space for the community, Alejandra founded Cat'te Bakery 24 years ago. As our head baker, Alejandra brings years of experience, creativity, and a love for tradition to every recipe.

Kitchen Team

Behind the scenes, our talented kitchen team works tirelessly to bring our recipes to life. From mixing and baking to decorating and packaging, each member of our kitchen crew plays a vital role in delivering fresh, delicious baked goods to our customers every day.

Front-of-House Team

Our front-of-house team is here to make your experience at Cat'te Bakery truly special. Whether you're placing an order, seeking recommendations, or simply stopping by for a chat, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist you with a smile.
